Our Story

Building Zimbabwe,
One Tile at a Time

Nice Perfect was founded in 2019 with one simple goal — to bring world-class tile installation to homes and businesses across Zimbabwe. What started as a small team with big dreams has grown into one of the country's most trusted names in professional tiling.


We believe that every surface tells a story, and we are committed to making that story beautiful. From elegant bathroom walls to stunning kitchen floors, we approach every project with precision, care and an eye for detail. At Nice Perfect, we don't just install tiles — we create spaces people love.
